HOW TO PLAY JUNGLE

Your guide on how to play jungle

In League of Legends, the Jungle is a role that involves navigating the neutral territory between the lanes, clearing camps for gold and experience, and ganking or supporting the other lanes.

CHOOSE A CHAMPION

Mastering jungle champs

There is a wide variety of champions that can be played in the Jungle, each with their own strengths and weaknesses. Some popular Jungle champions include Lee Sin, Nidalee, and Elise.If you're new to the game, it's best to choose one champion and focus on mastering their abilities and playstyle. This will help you build a solid foundation and improve your overall performance in the jungle.

SUMMONER SPELL SELECTION

Optimal Choices for Your Role

In League of Legends, junglers typically take Smite as it's key for camp clearing and securing objectives. The second spell varies: Flash for versatility in escapes and engages, Ghost for movement speed to stick to targets, Exhaust to reduce an enemy's damage and speed, or Ignite to secure kills and reduce healing.

FIRST CLEAR

Jungle clear paths

Jungle pathing in League of Legends refers to the route that a jungler takes through the jungle to efficiently clear camps and gain experience and gold. There are various jungle pathing strategies, but here are some common ones:

For a safe jungle clear, start with your team's bot side: on Red team, begin with Blue Buff, move to Gromp, Wolves, Raptors, Red Buff, and end with Krugs; on Blue team, start with Red Buff, then Krugs, Raptors, Wolves, Blue Buff, and finish with Gromp, providing a complete and safe jungle clear for both sides.

For an aggressive, risky clear aiming for an early bot gank: on the Blue Team, start with Red Buff, swiftly move to Krugs, then immediately look for a gank bot lane; similarly, on the Red Team, start with Blue Buff, head to Gromp, and then quickly gank bot lane, exploiting any opportunities for early dominance at a higher risk.

RIFT SCUTTLER SPAWN

Securing the River's Bounty

The Scuttle Crab is a highly significant objective in the jungle, providing vision, bonus experience, and gold. It respawns every two minutes and becomes a contested area between junglers, making it a priority for securing advantages and contributing to successful ganks and overall map control. Laners' support is often crucial in helping the jungler secure this valuable resource.

GANKING

Help your team get ahead for an easier way to victory

Ganking as a jungler in League of Legends involves ambushing enemies in their lanes to help your team secure kills or apply pressure. Successful ganks require good map awareness, timing, and coordination with your team. Always look for opportunities where enemy champions are overextended, low on health, or when your allies can help with crowd-control abilities.

OBJECTIVES

Key Goals for Victory

As a jungler in League of Legends, your primary role revolves around securing key objectives to gain map control and provide buffs for your team. This includes securing Dragons to achieve powerful Dragon Souls, claiming the Rift Herald for turret advantages, controlling Baron Nashor to enhance pushing potential
