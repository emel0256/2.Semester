WHAT IS LEAGUE OF LEGENDS?

"League of Legends" is a team-based online game where players aim to destroy the enemy's base using unique characters and strategic gameplay.

"League of Legends" is a multiplayer online battle arena game developed by Riot Games. It pits two teams of five players, each controlling a unique character, against each other. The primary objective is to destroy the enemy's main structure, the Nexus, while defending your own. The game emphasizes strategic gameplay, individual skill, and teamwork.
