HOW TO PLAY ADC

Your guide on how to play adc

In League of Legends, the ADC (Attack Damage Carry) is a role played in the Bot lane and is responsible for dealing damage to the enemy team in team fights and taking down enemy structures such as towers and inhibitors.

CHOOSE A CHAMPION

Mastering adc champs

There is a diverse selection of champions that can be played in the ADC (Attack Damage Carry) role, but the most common ones are marksmen known for their ranged attacks and sustained damage. Some popular ADC champions include Jinx, Ezreal, and Kai'Sa. If you're new to the game, it's best to choose one champion and focus on mastering their abilities and playstyle. This will help you build a solid foundation and improve your overall performance as adc.

LANE TRADING

Understanding the Art of Damage Exchange

Auto-attacks in League of Legends are key for lane trading, helping to chip away at opponents' health and prompting defensive play.

In lane trading, using spells effectively means considering factors like cooldowns, costs, damage potential, opponent's position, and health to gain an edge and secure kills.

Knowing your own and your opponent's ability cooldowns is key to deciding when to trade lanes or initiate fights.

WARDING

Mastering the Map through Sight

Warding in League of Legends is the act of placing vision-providing items known as wards on the map. These wards reveal the surrounding area and provide crucial information about enemy movements.

WAVE MANAGEMENT

The Art of Wave Management in League of Legends

Wave management in League of Legends involves strategic control of minion waves to gain advantages, such as freezing to deny farm, pushing for map pressure, and slow pushing to set up dives or sieges. It's a vital skill for laning and overall game control.

SUPPORT YOUR JUNGLER

Help your jungler get ahead and secure objectives

As an ADC, supporting your jungler with objectives like Dragon is vital for your team's success. Prioritize rotating to the Dragon pit with your team before it respawns, establish vision control with wards and Control Wards, and position yourself safely but close enough to deal damage during the Dragon fight.
