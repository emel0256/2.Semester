WHERE TO START AND HOW TO PLAY

HOW TO PLAY

A beginners guide to League of Legends

League of Legends (LoL) is a fast-paced strategy game where two teams of five players each, aim to destroy the opposing team's nexus, located within their base. Players start by choosing a champion, each with unique abilities. Once the game begins, earn gold by killing enemy units, and use it to buy items and boost your champion's power. The game map is divided into lanes, where most of the combat happens, and a jungle area filled with neutral monsters for additional resources. Teamwork, strategy, and quick decision-making are key to winning in LoL.

CHOOSE YOUR GAME MODE

Engage in strategic combat in 'Summoner's Rift' or enjoy relentless fights in 'ARAM'

'Summoner's Rift' is the classic, competitive mode of League of Legends. In this 5v5 mode, two teams strive to destroy the enemy's nexus, located at the heart of their base. The map features three lanes - top, mid, and bot, along with a jungle filled with monsters. Strategic planning, timely execution, and team synergy are the keys to triumph.

'ARAM' (All Random All Mid) simplifies the League of Legends experience into a single lane of non-stop action. It's a 5v5 mode where players are randomly assigned a champion. The relentless fighting tests your ability to make split-second decisions and teamfight effectively. With no respawns until your team is wiped, each move counts.

Summoner's Rift game modes

Choose your Summoner's Rift game mode!

In 'Summoner's Rift', you can choose from 'Blind Pick' for casual play, 'Draft Pick' for strategic champion selection, 'Ranked Solo/Duo' for competitive play with a partner or alone, or 'Ranked Flex' for team-based ranked matches.

What lane to play

Choose your lane!

'Summoner's Rift' in League of Legends consists of three lanes and the jungle. 'Top' lane is usually taken by resilient tanks or bruisers. 'Mid' lane is often occupied by bursty mages or assassins. 'Bot' lane is generally shared by a damage-centric ADC and their supportive partner. The 'Jungle' is home to the Jungler, who focuses on neutral monster camps, helping lanes by 'ganking' (surprise attacking), and securing crucial map objectives.

Buy items

Buying the right items, can win you the game!

In League of Legends, items enhance your champion's abilities, providing extra power, defense, or utility. Purchased with gold earned during the game, they can drastically impact your performance. The choice of items should align with your champion's role, the flow of the match, and the composition of the enemy team.

Starter

Starter items are initial purchases that provide early-game benefits tailored to specific champion roles and playstyles, enhancing their effectiveness during the laning phase.

Consumables

consumables are temporary items that champions can purchase and use to gain immediate benefits during the match, such as health restoration, vision control, or other buffs.

Trinkets

trinkets are a type of consumable item that provides additional utility to champions, allowing them to place wards, reveal hidden areas, or enhance their vision control throughout the game.

Distributed

Distributed items primarily refer to items that are granted to players during the game through specific mechanics or events.

Boots

Boots are in-game items that increase a champion's movement speed, offering enhanced mobility and positioning during matches. Each pair of boots also offers a specific buff like extra armor.

Basic

Basic items are fundamental in-game components that champions can purchase and build upon to create more powerful and specialized items.

Epic

Epic items are powerful and advanced in-game items that provide significant boosts to a champion's abilities and stats. These items are usually more expensive and take longer to complete

Legendary

Legendary items are top-tier, high-impact items that significantly augment a champion's abilities and strength, often granting powerful passives or actives.

Mythic

Mythic items are the most influential and game-changing items that champions can acquire. Each champion can have only one Mythic item in their build, and these items provide unique and powerful effects

Take objectives

Take objectives to assist your team with gold and power. Conquere the objectives alone or with your team!

Objectives in League of Legends are crucial strategic elements that can swing the game's momentum. 'Towers' defend lanes and provide map control; destroying them grants gold and advances your territory. 'Inhibitors' spawn powerful minions when destroyed, aiding in sieges. 'Dragon' and 'Baron Nashor' are major neutral monsters that grant significant team-wide buffs when defeated. 'Elder Dragon' and 'Baron Nashor' can especially become key late-game objectives, providing enhancements that can clinch victories.

Towers

Take towers to assist your team with gold and power.

By coordinating with your team, using strategic positioning, and pushing minion waves, you can systematically take down enemy towers and gain map control. Destroying towers provides the team with gold, opens up the map, and creates opportunities to siege the enemy base, ultimately leading to victory.

Nexus

Destroy the nexus and win the game

In League of Legends, the Nexus is the ultimate objective and core building located at the heart of each team's base. It represents the key to victory in every match. The primary goal for players is to destroy the enemy Nexus while protecting their own. Once a team successfully takes down the enemy Nexus, they achieve victory in the game.
