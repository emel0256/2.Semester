HOW TO PLAY SUPPORT

Your guide on how to play support

In League of Legends, the Support role is played in the Bot lane and is responsible for providing utility, protection, and crowd control for their team, especially for the ADC.

CHOOSE A CHAMPION

Mastering support champs

In the support role, there is a diverse selection of champions, but the most common are enchanter supports, tank supports, and engage supports. Some popular support champions include Janna, Leona, and Thresh. If you're new to the game, it's best to choose one champion and focus on mastering their abilities and playstyle. This will help you build a solid foundation and improve your overall performance as support.

LANE TRADING

Understanding the Art of Damage Exchange

Auto-attacks in League of Legends are key for lane trading, helping to chip away at opponents' health and prompting defensive play.

In lane trading, using spells effectively means considering factors like cooldowns, costs, damage potential, opponent's position, and health to gain an edge and secure kills.

Knowing your own and your opponent's ability cooldowns is key to deciding when to trade lanes or initiate fights.

WARDING

Mastering the Map through Sight

Warding in League of Legends is the act of placing vision-providing items known as wards on the map. These wards reveal the surrounding area and provide crucial information about enemy movements.

ENGAGE OR PEEL

Engage on the enemies or peel your allies

In League of Legends, "engage" involves initiating fights, while "peel" focuses on protecting teammates from enemy threats during team fights. Both strategies play vital roles in determining the outcome of battles.

ROAMING

Your Impact Across the Map

Roaming as a support is a crucial aspect of your role in League of Legends. Look for opportunities to roam when your ADC is safe and the wave is pushed or under control. Prioritize vision control with wards to roam safely, and consider assisting your jungler or roaming to the mid lane to turn skirmishes in your team's favor or secure kills for your teammates.

SUPPORT YOUR JUNGLER

Help your jungler get ahead and secure objectives

Supporting your jungler as a support is essential for your team's success. Place wards strategically around jungle entrances and major objectives, and assist your jungler during invades or skirmishes. Be prepared to rotate and provide assistance in securing objectives or countering enemy ganks. Set up vision for upcoming objectives like Dragon or Baron and position yourself in team fights to protect your jungler or provide crowd control for the enemy team.
